Adaptive Solar MPPT Using Embedded and Matlab for Solar Applications

Introduction

Solar power continues to gain importance as a sustainable and environmentally responsible energy solution due to the increasing global demand for clean power generation.
Although photovoltaic (PV) systems provide renewable electricity, their performance is strongly dependent on the angle at which sunlight strikes the PV panel.

Methedology

A dual-axis tracking system is implemented using an STM32 microcontroller. Four Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) are positioned in a cross pattern to detect illumination from different directions.
Based on the sensed light intensity difference, the microcontroller generates appropriate PWM signals to drive two servo motors for azimuth and elevation adjustments.This continuous sensor-based positioning enhances panel exposure to incident sunlight, resulting in improved energy extraction
