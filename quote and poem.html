Albert Einstein's Warning

"I know not with what weapons World War III will be fought, but World War IV will be fought with sticks and stones."

-Albert Einstein

A Poem for Peace

When the last great war has ended,
And the fires have cooled to grey,
What remains when all is mended?
Only stones along the way.

Not for throwing, not for fighting,
But to build what we tore down.
Hands that once held weapons, writing
Hope upon the battleground.
